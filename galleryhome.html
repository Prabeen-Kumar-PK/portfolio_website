Career:

My photography journey starts in the year of 2011 from gallant studio,Gwalior,Mandhya Pradesh. After 4 year, when I have gained experience in photography field I moved to mumbai for betterment, and to have my dream


After I moved to mumbai ,I worked under Ravendra singh Bhadoria sir for 3 years.In that duration of time I had shot few flims stills photography as well as BTS for Ravendra sir ; like "Pareeksha", "BYPASS ROAD", "Laxmi Bomb", "Kotigobba 3(South film)",etc.


At the same time, I had worked with Hemant Pawar sir as a freelancer photographer for few movies like "Paani", "school College Ani life " , "Annya", "ye re ye re Paisa 2","Atkan chatkan",etc.


as a frelance photographer I have done some work like "funtar", "Luckee",etc.


For me Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever… It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything.
